Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze customer demographics, purchasing patterns, subscription trends, and revenue performance. The dashboard helps businesses gain actionable insights into customer preferences, spending behavior, and sales performance across product categories and age groups.

🎯 Objectives
Understand customer purchasing behavior.
Analyze revenue and sales trends across product categories.
Evaluate customer subscription adoption.
Identify high-value customer segments.
Support data-driven business decisions.
